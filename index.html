They say life is a cycle

Birth, growth, and ultimately, death.

Though this cycle may be long,

They teach us to love the time spent in it.

But how can I,

when all I see is a cycle of work and duties to the world

Years toiling under a blue sky,

the sun just out of reach

One of these days I’ll learn to step off the staircase of life

Into the harsh desert away from society.

It won’t be the end of a life,

But the beginning of a new one.
